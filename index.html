How long does company registration take?
A Private Limited Company typically takes 10–15 working days. This includes DIN/DSC generation, name approval, MOA/AOA drafting, and certificate of incorporation issuance.
What documents are required for trademark filing?
You'll need a PAN card, Aadhaar, your logo or wordmark in JPEG format, and your business details. Our team guides you through the complete checklist on sign-up.
Are government fees included in the pricing?
Our pricing clearly separates platform fees from government fees. All applicable government charges are displayed transparently before you confirm any service.
Can I track my service progress online?
Yes. Every service includes a real-time status tracker in your client dashboard. You'll also receive email and SMS updates at each milestone.
Do you provide consultation support?
Absolutely. Book one-on-one consultations with startup lawyers, property lawyers, tax consultants, and trademark experts directly through the platform.
What is your refund policy?
We offer a 60-day refund on our platform fee if you're not satisfied — no questions asked. Government fees paid to authorities are non-refundable.
